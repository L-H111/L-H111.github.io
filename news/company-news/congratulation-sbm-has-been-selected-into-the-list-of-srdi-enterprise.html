CONGRATULATION SBM Has Been Selected Into The List Of SRDI Enterprise

Summary：CONGRATULATION SBM has been selected into the list of SRDI Enterprise

Shanghai Municipal Economic and Information Commission announced the list of SRDI Enterprises (enterprises which has the characteristics of Specialization,Refinement,Differential and Innovation) in Shanghai of 2021 from May 19th to 25th , 2022. SBM was successfully selected into the list of enterprises with its rich experience and innovation strength in the field of high-end equipment manufacturing.

SBM won this SRDI Enterprise, which means the government’s recognition for its specialization and innovation and the encouragement for its competitiveness and contributions.

SBM was set up in 1987 and has been rooted in the field of high-end equipment manufacturing for 35 years. SBM always takes “customer’s success is our success” as the growth value. From the perspective of customers, SBM firmly believes that technology can create long-term value for them. So SBM uses more than 3% of the annual revenue as research and development funds to polish the equipment. Now, SBM has already obtained more than 100 kinds of core independent intellectual property rights, the certification of “Shanghai Famous Brand Products” and “High-tech Enterprise”, and has participated in the preparation of a number of industry standards. In the future, SBM will continue to increase investment in research and development, update technology in the field of equipment manufacturing, upgrade equipment and improve technology. Besides, SBM will create greater value for customers by the cooperation with universities and research institutes!
