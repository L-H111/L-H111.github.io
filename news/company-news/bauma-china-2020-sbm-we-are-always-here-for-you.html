From Nov 24 to 27, the bauma CHINA 2020 was held at the Shanghai New International Expo Center (NIEC).

During this period, SBM brought its new high-end equipment to this big exhibition, and established cooperative relations with many brands on the spot.

SBM Series New Product Launch Conference

At bauma CHINA 2020, SBM has launched various new products including VU Tower-like Sand-making System, HGT Gyratory Crusher and other star products. Once the new products were launched, it attracted the attention of many customers on the spot.

On November 25, SBM, together with ZWZ Group, ZKH and other powerful enterprises, held a grand strategic cooperation signing ceremony to formally establish a cooperative relationship to promote the high-quality development of China’s mining equipment.

SBM establishes strategic partnership with ZWZ Group

ZWZ is the largest bearing company in China, which ranks China’s first in terms of comprehensive economic and technical indicators. This cooperation with ZWZ will provide high-quality core component support for SBM crushing and grinding equipment.

SBM establishes strategic partnership with ZKH

As an important manufacturer and service provider of aggregates equipment around the world, SBM will provide equipment and services to domestic and foreign customers with more efficient and high quality under the coordination of ZKH (a digital supply platform).

This cooperation signing conference also received the focus of China Aggregates Association, during the period, they delivered a speech.

“With more than 30 years of development, SBM has quickly become a leading enterprise in China’s high-end mining machinery industry. Today, SBM’s signing with two excellent suppliers is the beginning of a good cooperation. We hope that SBM and other aggregates equipment enterprises can work with together to promote the high-quality development of Chinese aggregates industry!”

In addition, at the bauma site, SBM signed three major aggregate projects.

SBM & Dongchen Building Materials Co-build Green Building Materials Comprehensive Industrial Park

The green building materials comprehensive industrial park project jointly built by SBM and Dongchen Building Materials, which covers an area of more than 200 acres, with a total investment of 360 million RMB. It is estimated that the annual production of aggregates can reach 10 million tons after the project completing, and the annual output value is estimated to be about 700 million RMB.

Shibang Group & Kenya Devki completed Online Contract

There is no doubt that 2020 is a troubled year. The COVID-19 spreads globally and the international trade encounters the black swan incident. However, SBM Group has got through them!

In this day, with the advantage of Internet, SBM reached the third cooperation intention through remote connection with Devki (a cement and building materials company giant Group in Kenya). This cooperation will refresh a new page for SBM.

Another EPC cooperation project is signed again after Dongyang’s

The Dongyang Aggregates Project is an important milestone event for SBM to help the construction of national green mines. The completion of the project is expected to promote the high-quality development of aggregates industry to make a major breakthrough!

Although in this special time, the epidemic has still brought various impact to everyone. But for SBM, we insist on the principle that epidemic prevention and development are both important. The development of China’s mining industry is inseparable from excellent partners.

We believe that as long as we work together under the premise of doing a good job in safety protection, we will be able to break through the barriers and move forward…
