About Us

Company Profile

Strong production bases, abundant manufacturing experience and professional research team help the in-depth development of mining machines. Both product types and models can meet all demands in this industry, guaranteeing the quality and delivery of equipment.

 

SBM Growth: Value + Values

As one of leaders of global crushing and grinding industry, SBM is always committed to innovation & excellence. Grasping strong production capability, advanced research strength and excellent service, SBM create the value and bring values to customers.

 

Independent R&D

3% of sales volume is put on R&D every year. Supported by computer-aided design systems — CAD and Solidworks, simulation analysis software — Ansy and matlab, programming software — VCC and EasyBuoder8000, SBM optimizes product research, design and production by every details to ensure accuracy, reliability and perfection of program formulation. Futhermore, SBM has integrated R&D, technical service, marketing and production to enhance R&D and innovation abilities and comprehensive competitiveness at home and abroad.

 

International Trade

SBM holds perfect international trade system, ranging from international trade marketing to international trade negotiation and from domestic one-stop service to overseas localization service. SBM’s international trade markets have been expanded across Asia, Africa, Latin America and other developing countries in succession and have been successfully extended into Europe, America and Australia as well. SBM is changing the way business is done.

 

Technical Service

SBM believes the value of brand, which originates from not only excellent products and solutions, but also considerate pre-sales & after-sales technical services. Thus, the team with hundreds of technical engineers is set up to provide acceptable solution for series of problems across project consultation, on-site surveys, sample analysis, program design, installation, commissioning and maintenance guidance, which ensure that every part is docking in pace with customers and reach customer’s requirement.

Production Base

SBM, the leading enterprise of Chinese crushing and grinding industries, has always been dedicated to the development of ore crushers, sand-making machines and industrial grinders in the past 30 years. SBM provides high-quality sand solutions and complete set of equipment for large scale projects such as expressway, railway and water &electricity. To fulfill the strategy of local manufacture and global distribution, SBM takes Asia as the core area to radiate toward the mid-to-high end customers worldwide. The product has the European CE Certification and global quality certification ISO9001:2000, etc.. In pursuit of excellence, SBM promises to always provide high-quality products, professional technologies and heartfelt service to help customers create more profits and improve ecological construction in practical actions.
