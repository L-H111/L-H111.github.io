Features Of The Mill Line Modified By SBM

1. Active carbon is the porous absorbent. So during transportation, storage and usage, water penetration must be prevented because water shall soak all active spaces after water penetration and the active carbon would become ineffective.

2. Material like tar etc. shall not be allowed to be brought into the active carbon bed during using active carbon to avoid blockage in the active space and lead the failure of absorption.

3. During storage and transportation, the active carbon is not allowed to contact directly with fire source to prevent accident.

4. Pulse dust collector with filmed punched felt pocket should be selected for better dust collection and removal effect.
