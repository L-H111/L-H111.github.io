Working Principle

CI5X Impact Crusher breaks materials with impact force. When materials enter the working area of hammer, they may be crushed under the high-speed shock and then thrown onto the impact device above the rotor for another crushing. Next, materials bounce from the lining plate to the working area of hammer again. This process repeats continuously until materials reach qualified granularity and are discharged. Adjusting the gap between the impact rack and the rotor rack can change the granularity and shape of finished products. Behind the impact plate is a spring safety device. When unbreakable materials enter the crushing chamber, the impact rack goes backwards to discharge unbreakable materials out of the crusher.
